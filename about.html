Bachelors of Technology (B.Tech)

Completed my under-graduate studies specialized in Information Technology from prestigious Anna university in the year 2014. I achieved an overall GPA of 3.63/4. The degree included major courses like Java, Web technology, Object Oriented programming, Software Project Management, Database Management Systems, Object Oriented Analysis and DesignDuring.

In the final year of my undergraduate studies, In a team of three, we built an app from scratch in android that helps people to test vision power deficiency and color blindness. It was built using Android SDK with backend database as DB2. The work was demoed live to more than 300 students and professors in my university and got published as a research paper in the IRAJ magazine (link). This project received recognition and got selected in the IEEE UKSim2014 conference at Cambridge University,London.

CAREER

AX Functional Consultant in Microsoft Dynamics

After my under graduate studies, I felt inspired to start developing software for real-world use cases. This led me towards taking up a position as Functional Consultant in the Microsoft Gold-Partnered Company, CEM Business Solutions. CEM Business Solutions is one of the fastest growing IT companies, providing implementation services in the Microsoft Dynamics practices. During my time at CEM, I learnt about Dynamics ERP software AX RU8 version modules like payroll, Human resource management and inventory warehouse management.

I got the opportunity to work on three USA and two UAE client projects as a functional consultant interacting directly with the client enterprise teams. Assisted the clients in defining business requirements and prepared functional design documents and worked with the technical team members and assisted them in preparing technical design documents.Coordinated and trained the Project Core Team members (Business Key Users) at the client site.Working on these projects made me realize the impact of software engineering in everyday life.
